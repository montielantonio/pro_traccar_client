Privacy Policy: GpsLink Client (Mobile Tracking)

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Explicit User Consent

This application is designed for voluntary tracking. The user must manually activate the tracking service.

Key Clause: "By activating tracking, you agree to share your real-time location with the GpsLink platform for logistics management, personal safety, or work supervision purposes."

2. Background Location Usage

GpsLink Client collects location data to enable real-time tracking of your position even when the app is closed or not in use. This allows your monitoring center to locate you in case of an emergency or logistical need.

3. Active Tracking Indicator

The app will always display a persistent notification or an icon in the status bar when tracking is enabled, so that the user is never tracked "secretly."

4. User Control over Data

The user has the right to turn off tracking at any time using the "Service Status" switch within the application.

5. No Data Commercialization

GpsLink does not sell or use customer location data for advertising targeting or third-party commercial profiling.
